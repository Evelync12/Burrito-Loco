How to play

Gameplay

-Burrito Loco is a trivia game where you must answer trivia questions correctly to level up and get the ingredients to form your burrito. Each level has questions from another catagory. Complete all levels and gather all ingredients to win!

Ingredients?

-Each level you complete will give you one ingredient to make your burrito, complete all 4 levels to make your complete burrito!

Levels?

-To level up, you have to answer 5 quesitons in a row to progress to the next level. If you get it wrong, you will restart from the beginning of your current level.
