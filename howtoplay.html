How to play

Gameplay

-Burrito Loco is a trivia game where you must answer trivia questions correctly to level up and get the ingredients to form your burrito. There are 2 categories to choose from, pop-culture and slogans.

Ingredients?

-Each level you complete will give you one ingredient to make your burrito, complete all 4 levels to make your complete burrito!

Levels?

-To level up, you have to answer 5 quesitons in a row to progress to the next level. If you get it wrong, you will restart from the beginning of the current level that you're on.
