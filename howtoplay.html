How to play

Gameplay

-Burrito Loco is a trivia game where you must answer trivia questions correctly to level up and get burritos. There are 4 categories to choose from.

Burritos?

-Burritos are used as in game currency to buy hints to help you answer the questions as they get harder

Levels?

-To level up, you have to answer 5 quesitons in a row to progress to the next level. If you get it wrong, you will restart from the beginning of the current level that you're on.
